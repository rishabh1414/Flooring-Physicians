Next-Gen Logistics Hub (FM2 Floor)

Industrial Logistics 150,000 Sq. Ft. Bengaluru

The client required an ultra-flat floor to support their new **Very Narrow Aisle (VNA)** system and automated guided vehicles (AGVs). We engineered an **FM2 tolerance slab** using 3D laser screed technology, ensuring minimal sway and high-speed efficiency for the robotic equipment.

The successful handover was three weeks ahead of schedule, validating our precise pour techniques and rigorous QA process.

  • System: Laser-Screed Concrete
  • Key Requirement: TR34 FM2 Flatness
  • Reinforcement: Steel Fibre Reinforced (SFRC)
  • Finish: Densified and Sealed

Luxury Automotive Showroom

Retail & Commercial 25,000 Sq. Ft. Mumbai

The brief was to deliver a sleek, modern aesthetic that could also withstand the weight of luxury vehicles and continuous foot traffic. We opted for a high-gloss **Polished Concrete** system treated with lithium-silicate densifiers.

The resulting floor is highly reflective, contributing to the showroom's ambient lighting, is entirely dust-proof, and requires only routine auto-scrubbing for maintenance, providing an ideal low-cost, high-impact finish.

  • System: Mechanically Polished Concrete
  • Key Requirement: High Gloss (3000-Grit)
  • Aesthetic: Integral Light Grey Dye
  • Protection: Chemical Densifier & Stain Guard

Food & Beverage Processing Plant

Specialized Industrial 55,000 Sq. Ft. Pune

In this facility, the floor must withstand frequent thermal shock from steam cleaning and acidic wash-downs, making hygiene paramount. We installed a heavy-duty **Urethane Cement** system with integrated cove base detailing.

Urethane cement is non-porous and highly thermal-resistant, eliminating microbial harbouring points and ensuring rapid, easy sanitation, complying with strict FDA and HACCP hygiene standards.

  • System: Urethane Cement Slurry
  • Key Requirement: Thermal Shock Resistance
  • Feature: Seamless Integration with Cove Base
  • Protection: Anti-Microbial Properties

Corporate Campus Outdoor Plaza

Architectural & Outdoor 30,000 Sq. Ft. Gurugram

The existing plaza was cracked and stained but the budget prohibited demolition. Our solution was a **Stamped Polymer Cement Overlay**. We applied a thin layer that masked the imperfections and stamped it to replicate an elegant European cobblestone pattern.

This method provided a cost-effective, durable, and slip-resistant aesthetic upgrade, bringing a premium feel to the high-traffic outdoor communal area in minimal time.

  • System: Polymer Cement Overlay
  • Key Requirement: Aesthetic Resurfacing
  • Pattern: European Cobblestone Stamp
  • Benefit: UV Stable and Slip-Resistant
